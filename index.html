Privacy Policy — NIGHTLANE RIVALS

Publisher: BDJ  ·  Contact: Bookings@bdjofficial.com

Last updated: 5 July 2026

Summary

NIGHTLANE RIVALS is a single-player arcade racing game. It has no accounts, no sign-in, no chat, and no in-app purchases, and it does not use your camera, microphone or precise location. We, the publisher, do not collect or store any personal information about you. The game does display banner advertising (on menu screens only, never during a race), which is served by Google AdMob and requires an internet connection.

Information the publisher collects

None. Your game progress (career results, coins, owned cars, upgrades and settings) is saved only on your device in a local file that is never transmitted to us. We run no analytics of our own and operate no servers that receive your data.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Ads are served by Google AdMob. To serve, cap and secure ads, Google may process limited technical data including your device's advertising identifier and general device information. We request non-personalised ads, meaning ads are not based on a profile of you. We show banner ads only, on menu screens; there are no full-screen, interstitial or rewarded ads, and no ads during gameplay. Google's handling of this data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy. You can reset or limit your advertising identifier in your device settings, and manage ad personalisation at Google Ad Settings.

Permissions and network

The app requests no sensitive permissions (no contacts, camera, microphone or precise location). It uses an internet connection only to load ads; all gameplay runs on the device.

Children

The game contains no chat, no purchases and no links out, and its ads are configured as non-personalised. It is intended for a general audience aged 13 and over and is not directed to children under 13.

Data deletion

NIGHTLANE RIVALS has no user accounts and keeps no personal data on any server, so there is no account or profile for us to delete. Your game progress lives only on your device and is removed if you delete the app. The only identifier involved in the app is your device's advertising ID, used by Google AdMob to serve ads; you can reset or delete it at any time in Android Settings → Privacy → Ads and manage ad settings at Google Ad Settings. To ask us anything about your data, email Bookings@bdjofficial.com.

Changes and contact

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this address before the change takes effect. Questions: Bookings@bdjofficial.com.
